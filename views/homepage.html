Health Risk Calc Home Page

Instructions:

This tool can be used to determine a person's coverage risk. Penalties are assighned for various conditions related to age, bmi, blood pressure, and family history.

You will be asked variety of questions about health.

  • Height is in feet and inches
  • Weight is in pounds
  • Blood pressure is measured using two numbers: systolic and diastolic.
  • You have to answer yes/no for family history
